Castle Rock Climbing Gym is an indoor rock-climbing gym located in South Lake Tahoe, CA. Our gym provides the Tahoe community the opportunity to rock climb no matter the time of day or condition of weather. Our mission is to inspire climbers to defy gravity and reach new heights.

Our climbing gym has several amenities including rock walls ranging from 10-100ft, bouldering, climbing equipment, yoga room, cycling class, and a sauna.

Climbers of all skill levels are encouraged to come! We have various routes that range from beginner to expert.
